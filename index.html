Research

My PhD is about modelling, learning and sampling high dimensional maximum entropy distributions. Along with my supervisor and co-authors, we have developped frameworks using both the renormalisation group and stochastic interpolants, with applications to out of equilibrium physical fields (including active matter and turbulence), finance and real world datasets.
